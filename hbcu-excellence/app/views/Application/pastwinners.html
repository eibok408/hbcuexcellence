About The Winner

The 2018 recipient of the Annie Ibok Scholarship is Ms. Tyra Roberson from Renton, Washington!

Tyra is a Business Marketing and Supply Chain double major, attending North Carolina A&T State University in the Fall. As a highschool student, she was the president of the Black Student Union and head leader of the business club "DECA". As student-athlete, she participated on her school's soccer and basketball team. Not only is she a dedicated student, she also has natural leadership skills, compassion for others, and a strong determination to manifest success in her own life. Tyra looks forward to attending her university, once believing that her college dreams were just out of reach. The Annie Ibok Scholarship will aid her in attending college without crippling student loan debt.
